Batch Processing In Chemical Plant

Batch processing involves selection of recipe, transferring relevant recipe to floor, maintaining parameters of each stage of the recipe, quality checks, transferring to various stages, packaging, filling, storage and dispatch. Solution ready package can take the order from ERP system, can schedule the order to corresponding unit analyzing the data and load of each unit, sends relevant set points for control at each stage, has many asset control inbuilt, monitors various raw material consumptions, generates indent intimation to ERP of corresponding raw material is in short supply, takes complete note of quality at each stage, optimizes energy and man power utilization, controls and monitors packaging and filling, monitors ware house management, gathers data of each stage of batch process and also sends relevant data to ERP.
