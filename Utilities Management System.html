Utilities Management System

Utility lines are the arteries of an industrial project. Monitoring, data analytics, intelligent decision making and controls of utilities make industrial project productive and avoids failures. UMS is a solution ready package, which manages assets, utilities and lines of all the utilities of plant from one place.UMS makes management of utilities ubiquitous as they can be accessed from any place and interoperate with any APIs resulting in quick decision making and early warning to avoid any manufacturing shut downs. It has in built management for following utilities, their assets and lines.

  • Boiler Management
  • Electrical System management-substation and Distribution
  • Air compressor and pneumatic systems
  • Effluent treatment plant
  • Industrial water systems
  • Water treatment plant
  • Chillers, cooling towers and pumps
  • Nitrogen generators
  • Diesel generator sets
  • Fuel Management
  • Lighting management
  • Solar generation management
