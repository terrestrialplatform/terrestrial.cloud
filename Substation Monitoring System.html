Substation Monitoring System

Solution ready package integrating various field devices on single platform integrating various devices, energy data, relays and real time signals. A holistic solution for smart grid IT backbone. It has inbuilt support for various substation control and monitoring protocols such as IEC 101/104, IEC 61850 or DLMS for meters. It is again device brand agnostic and is fully capable of integrating wired or wireless communications. It can be accessed from any device or operating system, in app or standalone form. It has all kinds of management features such as user management, roles management, device management, mimics management, dashboard management or low-code scripting. It also can analyze energy consumption data as well as energy generation data.
